- A **correlate** is “a phenomenon that accompanies another phenomenon & is related in some way to it” (Hartnagel, 2012)
# Do Churches and Ice cream cause crime?
◼ The more churches in a city, the more crime there is.
Thus, churches lead to crime.
◼ The more ice cream that is consumed, the more
crime there is. Thus, ice cream eating leads to
crime.
◼ third variable problem
